Camp Initiative Fund

The Camp Initiative Fund is dedicated to sustaining and expanding the impact of the Camp Initiative Grant. Through contributions from individuals, corporations, and foundations, the Fund will grow to enhance and supplement the support provided by the Rasmuson Foundation and other key partners.

Donate

The Alaska Fund

A fund to meet the ever-changing needs of Alaska, the Alaska Fund enables the Alaska Community Foundation to address emerging needs across the state, engage in collaborative philanthropy, and strategically award grants for the most significant impact.

ACF Annual Fund

Your gift to the Annual Fund ensures the Community Foundation will continue to inspire the spirit of giving to strengthen Alaskas communities now and forever.
